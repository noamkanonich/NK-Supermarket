2) The story of NK Super Market :

'NK Super Market' is a web application for costumers at the super market.
You can find in the application varius of different options like - shopping list, specific products lists, recent purchaces amounts and their dates,
about the NK Super Market application and gallery and contact us page.


3.1) Instructions :

- Login screen - you can log in to your account.
If you don't have one, it will send you to the Register page.

- Register screen - if you do not have an account, you will ask to register.
you need to enter - Name, Username, Password, Verify password.
After that, it will send you back to the login screen, and then you can login to your account.

- Home screen - When you login to your account, you will move to the ideas screen - The Shopping List.

- Shopping List screen - Here you can see your current shopping list, and add / delete / edit products from your shopping list.

- Specific Product Lists screens - Here you can choose between varius of specific products lists from your shopping list,
like - Vegetables, Fruits, Meat, Drinks and Other.

- Gallery screen - Here you can enjoy the super market gallery.

- About screen - Here you can read all about the NK Super Market application.

- Contact Us screen - Here you can send a message with your full name and phone number.

- Recent Purchaces screen - Here you can see all your recent purchaces amounts and all your purchases dates, and you can add / delete purchaces amounts to and from your list.
